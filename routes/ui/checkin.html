Check-In Resources

💧 Hydration
  • Drink a glass of water
  • Refill your water bottle
  • Choose water with your next meal
  • Add electrolytes after intense activity
  • Eat water-rich foods (watermelon, cucumber, oranges)
🍽 Nutrition
  • Eat a source of protein today
  • Include a fruit or vegetable with a meal
  • Don't skip meals if possible
  • Choose foods that give lasting energy
  • Fuel your body before and after exercise
❤️ Self-Care
  • Take a 5-minute break
  • Go outside for fresh air
  • Practice deep breathing
  • Stretch or move your body
  • Reach out to someone you trust
  • Journal your thoughts
  • Do one thing that brings you joy
