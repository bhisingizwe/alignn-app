Mood Resources

🌱Grounding exercises

👀 5-4-3-2-1 Technique

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

💪Progressive Muscle Relaxation

  • Tense a muscle group for 5 seconds
  • Relax for 10 seconds
  • Move through the entire body
😮‍💨Breathing support

🌬️Box Breathing

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds
  • Exhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 4 seconds

😌4-7-8 Breathing

  • Inhale for 4 seconds
  • Hold for 7 seconds
  • Exhale for 8 seconds
💭Mood reflection prompts

😊 Happy: What contributed to this feeling today, and how can I create more moments like this?

😔 Sad: What do I need most from myself right now?

😰 Stressed: What is one thing I can let go of today?

😤 Frustrated: What is this situation trying to teach me?

😟 Anxious: What is within my control right now?

😕 Overwhelmed: What is the next smallest step I can take?

😴 Tired: What would help me recharge today—physically, mentally, or emotionally?

🤔 Uncertain: What do I know for sure in this moment?

💪 Motivated: What action can I take today that moves me closer to my goal?

🙏 Grateful: What is something I appreciate today that I might normally overlook?

🌱 Neutral: How am I really feeling beneath the surface?

❤️ Loved: What made me feel supported, connected, or cared for today?

😞 Lonely: What kind of connection am I needing right now?

😠 Angry: What boundary feels crossed or unmet right now?

😌 Calm: What is helping me feel grounded in this moment?

🎉 Excited: What am I looking forward to most, and why?

😨 Fearful: What am I afraid might happen, and how likely is it?

🌤 Hopeful: What is giving me hope right now?

😔 Disappointed: What expectation wasn't met, and what can I learn from it?

🏆 Proud: What accomplishment or effort am I most proud of today?
