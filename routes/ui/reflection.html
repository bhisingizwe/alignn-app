💭 Guided Reflection Prompts

❤️ Emotional Processing Support
  • Name the emotion without judging it.
  • Notice where you feel it in your body.
  • Remind yourself emotions are temporary.
  • Ask yourself what this emotion may be trying to tell you.
🌱 Grounding Questions
  • What are 3 things I can see right now?
  • What is one thing I can hear?
  • What is one thing I can touch?
  • What is within my control today?
